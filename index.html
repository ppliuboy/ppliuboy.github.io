Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu and Prof. Irwin King. Before that, I got the B.Eng. degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning and machine learning.

Our 3D face reconstruction paper is accepted by ACCV 2020 as oral presentation.

Our Flow2Stereo is accepted by CVPR 2020.

Our SelFlow paper is selected in the CVPR 2019 Best Paper Finalist. SelFlow is the 1st place winner on Sintel Optical Flow Benchmark from November 2018 to November 2019.
